
------------------ for the spinach tortilla (2)
100g		eggs
220g		spinach
4g			aromat
1g			veg oil
													Total (220g)

Wilt the spinach in a pan with 50g water & lid
Add the aromat an mix in once wilted, 
Remove lid and keep on the heat until all the water has evaporated.
Beat the egg and add in the spinach.
Coat pan with oil, and drain to leave super thin coat of oil.
Pour in the mix, cook like normal omelette.
Toss, finish other side and turn out.
